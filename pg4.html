mantain distance from your phone


Phones in general can easily distract you. The best thing that can be done to avoid distraction is to put the phone on silent, turn the notification off and flip it over so that you don’t look at your phone now and then. Or if this also doesn’t help it’s better to switch off your phone. An even better idea is to keep your phone out of sight to avoid the temptation to check your messages.
